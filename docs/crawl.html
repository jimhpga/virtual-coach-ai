How to Hit It Farther
  1. Step-Through Speed Drill (10 reps × 3 sets) — Make a normal backswing, then step your trail foot toward target as you start down. Focus on fast, late speed. Rest 30–45s per set.
  2. Overspeed Swings (6 reps × 3 sets) — Three swings at 105–110% effort with a light club/shaft, then three with your gamer. Measure/contact optional; chase speed safely.
  3. Medicine-Ball Rotational Throws (8 reps × 3 sets) — Chest-high, throw into a wall/ground with hips leading. Feel ground up sequence. (No wall? Do banded rotations.)

Do 2–3 days/week, non-consecutive. Warm up first. Stop if you lose balance or form breaks.
