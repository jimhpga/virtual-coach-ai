How to film (best results)

  • Angles: Take Face-On and Down-the-Line (both if possible).
  • Height & distance: Camera at roughly hip height, about 8–10 feet away.
  • Keep the whole swing: Make sure the entire clubhead, hands and feet stay in frame.
  • Stability: Tripod/bag clip or prop the phone; avoid zoom; 60 fps helps.
  • Lighting: Avoid strong backlight; bright and even lighting helps the tracker.

Pro tip: record two swings per angle—if one is shaky, you’ve got a backup.
