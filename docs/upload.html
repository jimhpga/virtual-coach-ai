Face-On Setup

  • Camera ~hip height (belt buckle).
  • 8–12 ft away; level with the stance line.
  • Keep the entire club & hands in frame.
  • Lock focus/exposure if possible.
  • 60–120 fps is nice, normal phone video is fine.

Down-the-Line Setup

  • Place camera just outside the target line.
  • Same distance: about 8–12 ft.
  • Level horizon—avoid diagonal tilt.
  • Tripod, bag clip, or a steady friend helps.
  • Ensure the clubhead stays visible through impact.
