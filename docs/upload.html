Face-On Setup

  • Camera around hip height (belt buckle).
  • 8–12 feet away; level with the stance line.
  • Keep the entire club and hands in frame at all times.
  • Lock focus/exposure if your phone allows.
  • 60–120 fps is nice, but normal phone video works.

Down-the-Line Setup

  • Stand the camera on/just outside the target line.
  • Similar distance: about 8–12 feet.
  • Level horizon—avoid tilt/diagonal angles.
  • Tripod, bag clip, or a steady friend = better results.
  • Make sure the clubhead stays visible through impact.
