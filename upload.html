Best Results Come From Good Footage

Results are estimates from a single-camera video. For best accuracy, record from ~8–12 feet either down-the-line (along the target line) or face-on (perpendicular to target line), in landscape, with your whole body and club in frame.

Recording tips (quick)
  • Steady phone (bag stand/tripod), no zoom; 60–120 fps if available (30 fps is fine).
  • 3–10 seconds total; include the full swing.
  • Good light, no people crossing in front; show the ball & target line.
