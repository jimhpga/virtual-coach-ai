About Virtual Coach AI

Jim Hartnett is a PGA professional with over 30 years of experience, having taught at some of the top golf facilities in the world, including Kapalua, PGA West, Sahalee Country Club, and more. He has worked with renowned instructors like Jim McLean and studied the methods of the top teachers in the game.

Jim has spent decades refining his teaching, using video analysis, biomechanics data, and years of practical experience. Now, with the power of Artificial Intelligence, he’s able to bring a full tour-player coaching experience to every golfer — not just the pros.

Virtual Coach AI doesn’t just record swings — it analyzes every checkpoint (P1–P9) with biomechanical precision, finds inefficiencies, and prescribes drills tailored to the player’s body and goals. AI can spot patterns and micro-movements that most instructors simply can’t see in real time, giving every golfer the insights once reserved for tour players.
