About Virtual Coach AI

Why this exists

Golf instruction has evolved from feel and analog film, to launch monitors, 3D motion capture, and now AI-assisted analysis. Our goal is to make the best parts of modern coaching — clarity, consistency, and accountability — available to everyday golfers in seconds.

A (very) short history of instruction

  • Early era: teachers relied on ball flight and “feel vs. real”.
  • Video era: VHS and then digital slow-motion made checkpoints teachable.
  • Data era: launch monitors + force plates quantified strike, path, and pressure.
  • AI era: fast, consistent screening so golfers spend time on the right pieces.
