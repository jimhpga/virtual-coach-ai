About Virtual Coach AI

Jim Hartnett, PGA

Founder · Coach

Jim Hartnett has been a PGA professional for more than 30 years, working at top facilities including Coppola, PGA West, and Sawley Country Club, among others. He has had the pleasure of working with players who’ve won every major championship, and he’s coached golfers from brand-new beginners to tour winners. Jim has authored three golf books and continues to teach with a clear, there is a best swing that fits each individual fundamentals-first approach.

Virtual Coach AI turns that philosophy into fast, practical feedback using the P1–P9 checkpoints, so players at every level can improve with clarity, confidence, and purpose.
