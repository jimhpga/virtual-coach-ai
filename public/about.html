About Jim Hartnett

Coach Bio

Jim Hartnett has been a PGA professional for more than 30 years, working at some of the top golf facilities, including Coppola, PGA West, and Sawley Country Club, among others. He’s had the pleasure of working with players who have won all major championships, and has coached everyone from brand-new golfers to advanced competitors. Jim has written three golf books and continues to develop practical, no-nonsense systems to help players improve faster.

Teaching Philosophy

Keep it simple. Measure what matters. Build dependable motion one checkpoint at a time (P1–P9), then add speed. Virtual Coach AI reflects that philosophy—clear checkpoints, focused priorities, and shareable plans.
