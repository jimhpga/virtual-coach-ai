About Virtual Coach AI

Virtual Coach AI is the future of golf training — combining advanced biomechanics, artificial intelligence, and pro-level swing analysis into one powerful platform. Whether you’re a beginner or a tour-level player, our mission is simple: give you the tools to make better decisions, hit better shots, and lower your scores.

Our system analyzes your swing in real-time, compares it against elite players, and delivers actionable feedback with precision. But this is more than just swing data — Virtual Coach AI also tracks your mental game, course management, and long-term performance trends.

What Makes Us Different

  • AI-driven biomechanical analysis for every swing checkpoint (P1–P9)
  • Mental game scoring and training modules
  • Personalized drills based on your swing DNA
  • Pro comparison overlays and side-by-side analysis

We’re building Virtual Coach AI to make elite coaching accessible to every golfer, everywhere. If it doesn’t help your game, it doesn’t ship. Period.
