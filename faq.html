Frequently Asked Questions

What is Virtual Coach AI?
A simple system that turns your swing video into a structured report (P1–P9 checkpoints), two consistency scores, and a 14-day practice plan you’ll actually do.
How do I upload my swing?
From the Home page, click Upload Your Swing. Follow the prompts—face-on and down-the-line videos work best.
What are P1–P9?
Nine common checkpoints from setup (P1) to finish (P9). Clear language, quick video references, and one actionable cue per checkpoint.
What do “Swing Consistency” and “Position Consistency” mean?
Position Consistency = how reliably you match each P-position. Swing Consistency = repeatability of motion patterns across swings (tempo, face, path, etc.).
Do the scores use real metrics?
Yes. We map measured/estimated metrics to each section (tempo, face-to-path, hand path, pressure shift, etc.). No fluff numbers.
What if I don’t improve?
That’s on us to simplify. Short blocks, clear constraints, and feedback loops (video + checkpoints) make change stick. If it doesn’t help your golf, it doesn’t ship.
